📜 Rules & Regulations
  1. 🏫 This competition is open only for students of a select set of schools in Bangalore.
  2. ⚖️ Judges' decision is final and binding.
  3. 📋 Students are allowed to participate only in the competitions pertaining to their group, defined based on their Standard in school – e.g., Standard 5 student in Group III.
  4. 👔 Uniform & school ID card/badge are compulsory.
  5. 👨‍👩‍👧 It is the responsibility of the children's parents / guardians to always escort them during the finals of the competition.
  6. 🚗 Parents / guardians will have to manage the pickup and drop of their children from / to the venue for the finals.
  7. 📢 The date for the finals and the prize distribution ceremony will be announced through the schools.
  8. 🆓 All events except the Fancy Dress competition are free for the students – participation fee for Fancy Dress is INR 100/-. Last date to register: July 25, 2026.
  9. 📦 All submissions are the property of ISKCON New Rajapur Jagannatha Dham and will not be returned.

📍 Offline · Prelims at school, Finals at temple

🎵 3.2 Music
📍 Offline · Finals only at the temple premises
  1. 🎶 Each school assembles one music team of 5 members — 3 singers + 2 instrumentalists (mridangam, kartal, violin, guitar, harmonium).
  2. ⏱️ Performance time: 3 minutes in the finals.
  3. 🎼 Choose one song by Sri Purandara Dasa:

🧑‍🎓 Group 4 – Std VII, VIII, IX & X
🧠 4.1 Quiz
📍 Offline · Prelims at school, Finals at temple
  1. 📝 Preliminary round: written quiz for 20 minutes at school.
  2. 👥 Top 3 students form a team to represent the school in the final round.
  3. 📚 Topics: Krishna Leela, Ramayana, Mahabharata & stories from Srimad Bhagavatam.
  4. 🏫 School's responsibility to inform the 3 winners about team formation.
