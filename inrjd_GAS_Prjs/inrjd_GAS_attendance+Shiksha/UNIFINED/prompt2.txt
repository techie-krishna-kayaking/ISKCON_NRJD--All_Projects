Authorization
Authentication and authorization should remain separate concerns.
Authentication verifies identity.
Authorization determines access.
Every protected action should verify the user's role before allowing access.
For example:

•
Admin pages require Admin authorization.
Owner-only features remain restricted where applicable.
Future roles should be easy to integrate with minimal changes.
