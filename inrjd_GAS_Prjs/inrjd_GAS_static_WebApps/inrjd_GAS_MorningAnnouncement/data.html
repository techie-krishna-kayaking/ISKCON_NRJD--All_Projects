The Ten Offenses to be avoided while chanting the holy names are as follows:

1st Offense: To blaspheme the devotees who have dedicated their lives in propagating the holy names of the Lord.

2nd Offense: To consider the names of demigods like Lord Shiva or Lord Brahma to be equal to or independent of the names of Lord Vishnu.

3rd Offense: To disobey the orders of the spiritual master.

4th Offense: To blaspheme the Vedic literature or literature in pursuance to the Vedic version.

5th Offense: To consider the glories of chanting Hare Krishna to be imagination.

6th Offense: To give some mundane interpretations to the holy names of the Lord.

7th Offense: To commit sinful activities on the strength of the holy names of the Lord.

8th Offense: To consider the chanting of Hare Krishna as one of the auspicious ritualistic activities offered in the Vedas as fruitive activities (Karma-kāṇḍa).

9th Offense: To instruct a faithless person about the glories of the holy names.

10th Offense: To not have complete faith in the chanting of the holy names and to maintain material attachments, even after understanding so many instructions on this matter. It is also an offense to remain inattentive while chanting.

Every devotee who claims to be a Vaishnava must guard against these offenses in order to quickly achieve the desired success which is KRISHNA PREMA!
